# Restaurant DineFlow — Objection Handling

## “Ye to normal billing software jaisa hai.”

Answer:

> “Normal billing software sirf bill banata hai. DineFlow POS ke saath Store Inventory, Recipe Costing, Stock Audit, Day Close, Guest CRM, Events, Rooms/Villas aur AI Copilot bhi deta hai.”

---

## “Hum already Petpooja/eZee use kar rahe hain.”

Answer:

> “Aap continue kar sakte hain, lekin DineFlow ka focus AI + Store + Rooms/Villas + Guest Self Ordering + Events + Custom workflows par hai. Trial mein compare karke dekh lijiye.”

---

## “Thermal print kaise chalega?”

Answer:

> “DineFlow mein Print Station, 80mm layout, Auto Print, USB/Windows default mode aur network IP printer setting hai. Browser/kiosk mode abhi use hoga, future EXE direct print bridge ke liye ready hai.”

---

## “Inventory ka kya fayda?”

Answer:

> “Inventory se pata chalega kya samaan aaya, kis rate par aaya, kis department ne kitna liya, wastage kitna hua, vendor payment kitna due hai, aur kaunsa item mehenga hua.”

---

## “AI kya karega?”

Answer:

> “AI owner ko daily sales, low stock, vendor dues, purchase suggestions, price changes, recipe cost aur event leads ke baare mein batayega. Guest AI booking/order/room-villa questions handle karega.”

---

## “Price zyada hai.”

Answer:

> “Agar system monthly ₹2000-3000 mein stock leakage, billing errors, cash mismatch aur reporting time reduce kar de, to software apni cost nikal deta hai. Trial mein use karke decide kar lijiye.”

---

## “Google automation free kyun nahi?”

Answer:

> “Google Drive/Calendar setup technical hai — service account, folder sharing, calendar permissions sab configure karna padta hai. Isliye one-time ₹1499 setup charge hai.”

---

## “Staff ko use karna mushkil hoga.”

Answer:

> “Waiter/Chef/Cashier ke liye role-wise My Tasks screen hai. Staff ko sirf wahi options dikhte hain jo unke kaam ke hain.”

---

## “Data safe rahega?”

Answer:

> “Restaurant ka data restaurant account se linked hai. Backup/restore option hai. Future EXE phase mein local backup + cloud sync model bhi add hoga.”

---

## “Trial mein kya milega?”

Answer:

> “Trial mein full features milenge taaki aap system ka real power dekh sako. Trial ke baad selected plan ke hisaab se features continue honge.”
