# Restaurant DineFlow — Pricing Guide

Pricing can be changed from Super Admin. This guide explains how to pitch plans.

## Trial Strategy

Give 3 days full-feature trial.

During trial:
- All features visible
- Owner experiences full DineFlow power
- After trial, Super Admin assigns plan

Pitch:

> “Trial mein aapko full system milega. Trial ke baad jo modules aapko useful lagenge us hisaab se plan continue kar sakte hain.”

---

## Starter Plan

For small restaurants starting with POS.

Includes:
- Tables
- Menu
- KOT
- Basic billing
- Staff PIN login
- Basic sales
- Print Station basic

Pitch:

> “Agar aapko sirf billing, table order aur KOT chahiye to Starter enough hai.”

---

## Pro Plan

For restaurants that need guest ordering and hospitality features.

Includes Starter +
- Rooms
- Villas
- Guest self ordering
- Combined billing
- Sales Summary
- Reviews
- Printer department mapping

Pitch:

> “Agar aap rooms/villas ya guest self ordering use karna chahte hain to Pro plan best hai.”

---

## Premium Plan

For serious restaurants needing control and automation.

Includes Pro +
- Store / Inventory
- Recipe costing
- Stock audit
- AI Copilot
- Google Drive/Calendar automation
- Events CRM
- Day Close
- Advanced reports

Pitch:

> “Premium plan un restaurants ke liye hai jahan owner stock, reports aur AI decisions se business control karna chahta hai.”

---

## Enterprise Plan

For larger operators.

Includes Premium +
- Up to 3 outlets/restaurants
- Custom print bridge / EXE roadmap
- Advanced staff permissions
- Dedicated onboarding
- Custom integrations

Pitch:

> “Enterprise un businesses ke liye hai jinke multiple outlets ya advanced workflows hain.”

---

## DineFlow Elite

For high-value clients.

Includes Enterprise +
- Priority customization
- Launch training
- Owner/cashier/store onboarding
- Future EXE/APK upgrade priority
- Advanced support

Pitch:

> “Elite plan mein hum system ko aapke exact workflow ke hisaab se tailor karte hain.”

---

## Google Automation Setup Charge

Google Drive/Calendar/Gmail setup is technical.

Suggested one-time setup charge:

```text
₹1499 one-time
```

Includes:
- Drive folder setup guidance
- Calendar sharing setup
- Service account setup
- Owner settings configuration
- Test booking sync

Pitch:

> “Google automation setup technical hota hai, isliye hum one-time ₹1499 mein complete setup kar dete hain.”
